About Me

I am a Robotics PhD student at Georgia Institute of Techonology, working with professor Sonia Chernova. My research focuses on semantic reasoning for robotics, including knowledge extraction, representation, reasoning, and applications in household robots. I am currently developing neural methods for reasoning with large-scale knowledge bases.
