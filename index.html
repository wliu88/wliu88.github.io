About Me

I am a Robotics PhD student at Georgia Institute of Techonology, working with professor Sonia Chernova. My research focuses on Semantic Reasoning for Robotics. Specifically, I am interested in making robots that can operate in diverse and changing environments, such as hospitals and homes, by leveraging knowledge extraction, representation, and reasoning. I am currently developing methods for large-scale knowledge base reasoning and semantic grasping.
